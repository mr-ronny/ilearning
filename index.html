Frequently Asked Questions

Why are Salesforce Certifications important?

Salesforce Certifications aren’t just ordinary certifications. They are recognized in the industry and verify that the person is skilled in a specific Salesforce field. There are many Salesforce Certifications available in various fields, for people who wish to be skilled in those areas or want to start a career in that field. Salesforce Certifications are available for IT as well as non-IT professionals.

How do I track my order?

You can track your order using the tracking number provided in your confirmation email. Visit our tracking page and enter your tracking number.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship internationally. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination country.

Can I change my order after placing it?

Once an order is placed, it is processed immediately. If you need to change your order, please contact our customer service as soon as possible.
